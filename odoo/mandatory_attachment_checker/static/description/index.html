Mandatory Attachment Checker

Stop audit findings before they start—enforce a clean attachment trail on every sales order, purchase order, invoice, and expense. Turn scattered “we forgot the receipt” moments into systematic financial compliance, without slowing teams down.

The challenge—and how we solve it

From reactive fire drills to proactive control at the moment of confirmation.

The problem

Orders, vendor bills, and expense lines often move forward with empty chatter: no PO scan, no customer PO, no receipt. When auditors or finance review the trail months later, missing evidence becomes rework, write-offs, or compliance exposure. Manual spot checks do not scale.

  • Broken document chains after go-live
  • Retroactive hunts for receipts under audit pressure
  • Inconsistent enforcement across teams and subsidiaries

The solution

Mandatory Attachment Checker is your automated compliance gatekeeper. Per-company toggles require at least one ir.attachment on the record before confirmation, posting, or expense submission—so policy is applied at the exact workflow step where it matters, with clear, user-facing guidance when something is missing.

  • Central configuration in Settings—no code changes per rule
  • Native integration with Sales, Purchase, Accounting, and Expenses
  • Professional errors that tell users what to fix, not just “access denied”

Key features

Designed for multi-company operations and real-world accounting workloads.

MC

Multi-company support

Policies are stored on res.company and evaluated per document’s company. Enable strict controls in one entity while leaving another flexible—ideal for shared databases and regional differences.

GL

Smart bypass for automated journal entries

Invoice posting rules apply only where is_invoice() is true—customer invoices, vendor bills, and related credit notes—not miscellaneous journal entries. Automation and period closings keep moving without false blocks.

4×

Granular control per document type

Independent switches for sales orders, purchase orders, invoices, and expenses. Turn on exactly the controls your control matrix requires—no all-or-nothing compromise.

How it works & configuration

Three steps from install to enforced policy.

1
Install the module from Apps after adding it to your addons path. Dependencies (account, sale_management, purchase, hr_expense) load the models this module extends.
2
Open Settings and locate the Mandatory attachments block. Enable the toggles for each document family you want to protect. Settings save to the active company—repeat for other companies if needed.
3
Use Odoo as usual. When a policy is on and a user tries to confirm, post, or submit without a file on the record, Odoo raises a clear blocking message asking them to add an attachment via the chatter (or expense attachment) and try again—no silent failures, no mystery errors.
